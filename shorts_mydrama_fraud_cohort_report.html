Where does fraud actually live?

Headline findings

  • Zone 5 is the cleanest. Half the fraud rate of Zone 4, and the gap holds at every cohort age we can compare — not a statistical fluke.
  • Zone 3 runs hot. ~45% above baseline across 7 of 8 mature cohorts. Volume is small (≈9% of Shorts orders) so impact is moderate, but signal is consistent.
  • Zone 4 carries the brand. 82% of Shorts/MyDrama VISA orders. Whatever shifts here moves the company number.
  • Zone 6 is too small to call. 2.5k orders, 31 alerts. One bad cohort (Mar 30, 4.93%) on 65 users is 3 alerts away from being normal. Hold judgement.

Source
Base: solid-analytics-test.solid_raw.enriched_orders (VISA, status IN settle_ok / auth_ok).
Attribution: payments_with_creative (ad_name LIKE 'SH_%').
Fraud: fraud_alerts (card_scheme = 'VISA').
Methodology
A user is bound to the cohort week of their first VISA subscription_initial order with Shorts attribution. All subsequent VISA settle/auth_ok orders from that user count toward the same cohort. Fraud rate = alerts / orders within the cohort. Mature cohorts: age ≥ 21 days at report time, accounting for the ~30–60 day lag of network fraud reports.
